Florida rapper who dared critics to confront him shot dead minutes later
Rollie Bands was shot once in the parking lot of his apartment building

Author of the article:Brad Hunter
Publishing date:Jul 26, 2022 • 1 day ago • 1 minute read • 16 Comments
Rapper Rollie Bands invited his haters to confront him. They did. And shot him to death.
Rapper Rollie Bands invited his haters to confront him. They did. And shot him to death. PHOTO BY ROLLIE BANDS /INSTAGRAM

Florida rapper Rollie Bands took great exception to people criticizing his beats.

Advertisement 2
STORY CONTINUES BELOW

Article content
In fact, he took to Instagram on Friday, inviting the naysayers to confront him at his Tampa Bay-area apartment complex.

Minutes after posting his challenge on the social media site, he was shot dead.

According to HipHopDX, Bands wrote on his Instagram story: “A lot of these n*****s know where I live at fr. I sleep in peace. If a n***a want smoke I’m at my crib in 5 minutes.”

And five minutes after the posting, Bands was shot once in the parking lot of his apartment building. He died not long after being rushed to a trauma centre.

The suspects — who detectives believe knew and specifically targeted the rapper — fled the scene in a vehicle. There were no suspect descriptions available.

“At this time, the shooting does not appear random, as the suspects and victim are possibly known to each other,” the Hillsborough County Sheriff’s Office said in the report.

It’s believed the rapper is survived by a daughter, whom he described as “the only person I live for.”

A Florida man who was shot by his stepson last month in self-defense was himself arrested this week for battery after leaving the hospital, according to multiple reports.

On June 26, William Zimmerman, 53, was blasted by his stepson in the middle of a violent attack on his mother, Local 10 reported.
